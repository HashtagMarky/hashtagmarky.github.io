Pokémon Labradorescent Lights

 

When my partner first told me they'd recently gotten into playing Pokémon Go, I was excited that they'd fallen for an interest of mine. However, they admitted to having next to no interest in purchasing a new console in order to play any of the mainline games, I knew I had my work cut out for me. Pokémon Labradorescent Lights is a labour of love, dedicated entirely to them. It revamps the Generation III experience in a whole new light, while providing new challenges for me as a rom hacker. Forked from danenders' pkedx, it has various features that improve gameplay, and expanded content to keep players occupied.

The Final Demo of Pokémon Labradorescent Lights has been released, including content up to the end of "Adventure Begins!". I hope it will allow you to get a taste of this enhanced but classic adventure in Hoenn. A BPS and UPS patch have been provided, for an easy user experience.

Please note; the demo experience does not allow for saved games or the use of 'easy mode'. Whilst playing the demo, there is increased EXP gain. To prevent spoilers, there are also restrictions to the access of some areas, story elements, movesets and evolutions.

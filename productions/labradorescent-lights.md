When my **partner** first told me they'd recently gotten into playing **Pokémon Go**, I was excited that they'd fallen for an interest of mine. However, they admitted to having next to no interest in purchasing a new console in order to play any of the mainline games, I knew I had my work cut out for me. **Pokémon Labradorescent Lights** is a labour of love, dedicated entirely to them. It revamps the **Generation III** experience in a whole new light, while providing new challenges for me as a rom hacker. Forked from **TheXaman**'s **pkedx**, it has various features that improve gameplay, and expanded content to keep players occupied.

Pokémon Stellar Spirits

 

After seeing the love my partner had for Pokémon Labradorescent Lights, I decided to delve back into rom hacking in order to produce Pokémon Stellar Spirits. As a prequel, Stellar Spirits delves into the origins of the relationships shown in Labradorescent Lights, while again providing an enhanced experience of a known region. Being developed alongside Pokémon Ikigai, Stellar Spirits also provided a provisional space to develop new features for it.

Experience Kanto as never before, with a new story and catching mechanics. Again built upon the works of danenders, Stellar Spirits provided enhancements to your playing experience.

Pokémon Stellar Spirits is still in development, upon release, this page will be updated.

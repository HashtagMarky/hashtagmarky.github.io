## Content Format

TikTok supports two formats relevant to this brand:

| Format | TikTok equivalent | Use for |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram Carousel | Photo slideshow | Book reviews, build progress shots |
| Instagram Reel | Video | Devlog walkthroughs, ROM hack clips |

**Slideshows are the primary format.** They require no re-editing from Instagram carousels and perform well on the For You Page. The BookTok community in particular engages heavily with photo slideshows.

### Slideshow Image Dimensions

| Ratio | Dimensions | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 9:16 vertical | **1080 √ó 1920 px** | Recommended ‚Äî fills the full screen, no black bars |
| 1:1 square | 1080 √ó 1080 px | Supported but shows with black bars top and bottom |

- Format: JPG or PNG
- Max file size: 20 MB per image
- **Use 9:16 wherever possible** ‚Äî square slides from Instagram carousels will letterbox on TikTok. Resize or reframe for TikTok if the content matters enough to warrant it, otherwise accept the bars.

## Caption Structure

TikTok captions are shorter than Instagram ‚Äî aim for 1‚Äì2 sentences max before hashtags. The algorithm reads captions but viewers rarely do, so the visual hook on slide 1 or the first video frame carries more weight.

```
[One sentence ‚Äî names the thing, no teaser]
#BookTok #PokemonRomHack #romhacking
```

Do not replicate the full Instagram caption ‚Äî trim to the essential hook and tags only.

## Hashtag Strategy

TikTok hashtags work differently from Instagram. Use 3‚Äì5 tags per post, all relevant. Do not use irrelevant trending tags ‚Äî TikTok's algorithm classifies your account based on tag consistency, same as Instagram.

**Tag sets by pillar:**

- Books: `#BookTok` ¬∑ `#SciFiBookTok` ¬∑ `#FantasyBookTok` ¬∑ + series/author tag when applicable
- ROM Hacking: `#PokemonRomHack` ¬∑ `#RomHacking` ¬∑ `#PokemonEmerald`
- Tech/Builds: `#RaspberryPi` ¬∑ `#DIYGameboy` ¬∑ `#BuildLog`

`#BookTok` is one of TikTok's strongest communities ‚Äî always include it for book content.

## What Performs Well

- **Strong slide 1** ‚Äî same logic as Instagram: stop the scroll. Cover image + title + one-line opinion.
- **Text on every slide** ‚Äî a large portion of TikTok is watched on mute. Every slide needs readable on-screen text.
- **Opinion-led framing** ‚Äî "this book is overrated" outperforms "this book is good." Specificity and a clear take drive engagement more than positivity.
- **Short** ‚Äî slideshows under 15‚Äì20 seconds perform better for discovery. Don't pad.

## Audience Notes

The TikTok audience for ROM hacking and BookTok is largely **separate from Instagram**. Cross-posting is not redundant ‚Äî it reaches different people. Do not assume Instagram followers will find the TikTok account or vice versa.

BookTok skews younger and more reactive than Bookstagram. Expect faster feedback and higher comment volume relative to follower count.
